# Demo Page - AI Consulting Agency

Create a professional, modern landing page for "AI Insights" - an AI consulting agency.

## Company Overview
AI Insights helps businesses implement AI solutions. We specialize in machine learning, automation, and data analytics for mid-sized companies.

## Page Requirements

### Hero Section
- Bold headline: "Transform Your Business with AI"
- Subheadline about practical AI implementation
- Primary CTA: "Get Free Consultation"
- Secondary CTA: "View Case Studies"
- Modern hero image or gradient background

### Services Section
Title: "Our Services"

3 main services with icons:
1. **AI Strategy Consulting**
   - Business analysis and AI roadmap
   - ROI-focused recommendations
   
2. **Machine Learning Solutions**
   - Custom ML models
   - Predictive analytics
   
3. **Process Automation**
   - Workflow optimization
   - Intelligent automation

### Social Proof
- 3 client testimonials with names and companies
- Logos of 4-6 well-known companies (fictional is fine)
- Key metrics: "500+ Projects", "98% Success Rate", "50+ Industries"

### CTA Section
- Strong call-to-action
- "Ready to start your AI journey?"
- Contact form or booking button

### Footer
- Company info
- Services links
- Contact: email, phone
- Social media icons
- Copyright

## Design Requirements
- Modern, professional design
- Blue/purple gradient color scheme
- Clean typography (sans-serif)
- Mobile responsive
- Accessible (WCAG 2.1 AA)
- Fast loading
- Professional imagery

## Technical Requirements
- Semantic HTML5
- Modern CSS (flexbox/grid)
- Smooth scroll navigation
- Mobile menu
- Form validation (if included)
- SEO optimized
